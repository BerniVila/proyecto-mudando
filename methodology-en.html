Methodology

A beautiful space changes your life

contact

To start, our clients send us a plan of the property and some images of the property. After knowing the concept, we value our design fees. Once accepted, we start the process.

briefing

Our success in any project is to guarantee that we meet the wishes and aspirations of our clients. For this, we dedicate our energy to understand their needs, their lifestyle or work style, and generally their expectations regarding their property. Then we document about the property or installation, taking note of the dimensions, the characteristics and the images that we will need to work, as well as the administrative and investment needs of our client. At the end of this phase we provide the first ideas in order to understand the essence of the project as well as the main characteristics.

concept

In this moment and already with a clear goal, we start with the design development, we concrete the distribution in plant, we represent our ideas graphically to their correct interpretation through 2D and 3D images with the support of physical samples of materials and finishes.

development

Once defined the concept and accepted by the client, we proceed to the development and the elaboration of budgets. In this phase we detail through planimetries the location of installations, the materials of each instance and develop all the furniture pieces that will be manufactured to the project. Finally we conclude the terms for the start.

execute

After the acceptance of all the phases, we proceed to the execution of the work. Internally we schedule a schedule with the planning of work times and the delivery of furniture and decoration pieces according to the terms agreed by the suppliers. During the execution of the work we look for that our designs are correctly done and within the agreed terms, so that our clients can enjoy their property with tranquility.
